What I Do

I shoot pictures that capture emotion, Whether my own or of the people in my surroundings. Every stroke of the brush tells a story, a story that I hope translates to every person that lays their eyes on my work. Although I love telling individual's stories as they are , I also love to tap deeper into my creative side to create and tell a story that I can envision. If I am to tell a story that I envision, you can also tell me your vision and let me bring it to life in pictures.

Who I Am

Hi, I am colin Rikhotso and I am a self-taught portrait and an events photographer from Tzaneen in Limpopo. What started as a hobby blossomed into a passion. Now I get to share my love with the world. I mainly offer my photography services in Tzaneen and surrounding areas. However, I am willing to travel to places beyond if one needs my expertise.

My Work

As part of my photography journey, I have learnt not to just click the shutter button on my camera, but to tell a story through the images I take to bring your story to life for you to come back later down the line to relive those moments.
